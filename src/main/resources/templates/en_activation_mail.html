Structures

The area is placed inside the stripe, which is highlighted in blue for better perception.


The structures help you to customize the presentation of content in your letter — one-piece, several columns, chess, etc.


Try it yourself as it’s simple!

Drag and drop a structure with two columns from the «Structures» section on the side panel below this block. Then, in the same side panel, try changing the background color. Experiment with the menu functions — copy, move, delete.

Basic Blocks

Excellent! You are doing great! Now it’s time to get acquainted with the blocks. They are presented in the side panel of the editor in the section with the same name.


Dragging blocks into structures, you fill the letter with content and give it a finished look.


Let’s take a closer look at the most popular blocks.

Picture

As you have already guessed — the block allows you to upload your pictures.


But that is not all!

You can edit the downloaded pictures using the built-in sticker library..


For example, upload an image, click on the «Edit» button and add a frame to it or attach a festive confetti.

Text

Change the font and styles of your text on the fly using a convenient formatting bar.

Button

Using our editor, you can easily create a button that will best match the design of your site: shape, color, size, fonts, indents and other tools at your disposal.


For example, change the button’s color to blue, edit the radius of the corners by 10 and drag the button to the width of the structure column.
